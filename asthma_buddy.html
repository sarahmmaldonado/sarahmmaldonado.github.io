Asthma Buddy App

For the Asthma Buddy app, my group and I had to work together to create prototypes for an app targeted towards children with asthma and their parents/guardians. My role in the group was designing and creating the prototypes as well as contributing the final report and powerpoint.
Designing a health-focused app meant I had to be user-centered, making decisions based on what would make the app genuinely useful and accessible. It also improved my communication skills, as I gathered feedback and translated it into actionable changes. Additionally, this experience built my resilience, helping me work through creative challenges and prioritize functionality while keeping the design simple and intuitive. Overall, this project strengthened my user-focused thinking, communication, and problem-solving skills.
